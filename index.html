About Our Family

Welcome to IEEE IAS ISIMG Student Branch Chapter, established in February 2024. In a short time, we've made a remarkable impact, building a thriving hub for future engineers, innovators, and leaders. Our mission is to drive technical excellence, empower professional growth, and pioneer innovative projects that shape a smarter, more connected world.



Our Goals

  • Sustainable Impact

    Driving projects that solve real-world challenges with sustainable technology.

  • Industry Connection

    Bridging the gap between students and industry professionals for better career opportunities.

  • Innovative Learning

    We provide hands-on workshops and training on cutting-edge technologies.

  • Leadership & Growth

    Empowering students with leadership roles and personal development opportunities.

  • Global Networking

    Connecting members with IEEE’s international community for collaboration and mentorship.

  • Innovation & Research

    Encouraging students to engage in research, hackathons, and competitions.

Info session: IAS

To enhance the promotion of our IAS ISIMG SBC, we are organizing an info session about the Industry Application Society and its numerous benefits and opportunities for students to get involved in!

EcoGuardians V1.0

EcoGuardians is an AI-driven initiative promoting sustainability through innovative technology solutions. It empowers participants to develop eco-friendly projects that address environmental challenges.

LinkUp

LinkUp is a networking event designed to connect students and professionals, fostering collaboration and knowledge exchange. It provides a platform for skill development, mentorship, and industry insights.

Big Hero 6 : AI Bootcamp

The "Big Hero 6" bootcamp was designed to introduce participants to the fundamentals of artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning (ML), and deep learning, led by expert instructor Miss Taima Yahyaoui.

Workshop UX/UI Design

The UX/UI Design Workshop provides hands-on training in user experience and interface design principles. Participants learn to create intuitive, user-friendly digital products.

Industrial Visit

The Industrial Visit offers students a firsthand experience of real-world industry operations. It provides insights into professional environments, modern technologies, and practical applications of engineering concepts.
