Who are we ?

We are a newly established Student Chapter, founded in February 2024 at the Higher Institute of Multimedia in Gabes. Since our creation, we have organized over 20 activities and events to promote the goals of the Industry Applications Society. Our mission is to enhance students' skills in IT and soft skills, preparing them for future challenges in the industry.

Our Vision

To empower students with technical, entrepreneurial, and leadership skills, fostering innovation and industry readiness.

Our Mission

To provide hands-on learning experiences, professional networking opportunities, and knowledge-sharing platforms that bridge the gap between academia and industry.

Our Goal

To develop a dynamic community where students can explore emerging technologies, enhance their problem-solving abilities, and contribute to impactful projects.
